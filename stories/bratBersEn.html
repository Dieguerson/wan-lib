Bratoslav Bersk

The Bandit Ranger.

Race Class
Human Ranger
Background
Criminal

Bratoslav was the first born of the Bersk family. Him, his parents and his 10 siblings always lived on the streets. Growing up he had a few close friends, and a relatively normal childhood, at least as normal as a street family can get.

Of course, seeing all the wealthy people ignore his existence and his family’s he became very resentful of authority and saw a life of crime as the best way of fighting against the oppression and tyranny of gold. So, for many years he devoted his life to stealing, eventually becoming part of a gang of highway robbers, as their sharpshooter.

By his 25th birthday, Bratoslav, had made an enemy of one of the greatest caravan masters of the region (the blame of that to be put on all the robbing of course), had fell in love, gotten married and had 2 children (Vladimira and Dragomir), and seen the worst tragedy of his life.

One day, on a walk in the forest with his wife, a wild creature jumped him unaware and knocked him out. We he came to, he found Iskra, his wife, dead and the creature feeding on her body. Consumed by rage and grief, Bratoslav slain the beast.

After returning to town, and a brief period of mourning, he had to make a choice, his life had to change, the children needed a stable life, away from danger. And that meant leaving behind his life as a criminal.

Job opportunities were low for a known robber, but his experience with ranged weapons, his hatred of wild predators, and his ability to calm and soothe animals, made him apt for a job as a caravan guard, which eventually led to an opportunity as a ranger.

As a caravan guard, Bratoslav had to work with Chestibor, his old nemesis, the caravan master. And after a few years together (not without threats, missed payments, insults and the lot), they became good friends.
