The Wandering Librarian

Hello, my name is Woric Leatherbound, and I'm a dwarf. I'm also a wandering librarian.

As a dwarf, I like things well done. As a librarian, I make sure that any hero's story is available to anyone who asks for it. As a wanderer I travel all roads, big or small, to meet new adventurers, heroes, families and anyone else that can benefit from my services.

So, I'll be seeing you on the road, adventurer.
